Droplet configurations provide flexibility to build, test, and grow your app from startup to scale. DigitalOcean provides a range of Droplet options that are built with one goal in mind: to ensure that no matter what you’re building or how you scale, you’ll have the right infrastructure to support you.

[Premium Basic](https://www.digitalocean.com/pricing/droplets#basic-droplets)

The most standard and flexible Droplet and configurable amount of memory

For simple or bursty apps: low-traffic web servers, blogs, discussion forums, CMS, small databases, dev/test servers, microservices, repository hosting, etc. Use Premium Droplets to ensure the use of modern processors from Intel and AMD.

[GPU Droplets](https://www.digitalocean.com/products/gpu-droplets)

GPUs are specialized droplets that deliver cutting-edge GPU power to accelerate deep learning, AI/ML training, complex graphics rendering, and high-speed video processing. For computationally intensive workloads, GPUs significantly reduce training times, improve application performance, and enable you to handle massive datasets. Push the limits of innovation with next-level performance for your most demanding workloads.
